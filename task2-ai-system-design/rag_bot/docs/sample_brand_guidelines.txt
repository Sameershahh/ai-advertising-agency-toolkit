VERDE BEAUTY — BRAND GUIDELINES

BRAND MISSION
To prove that certified organic skincare can outperform conventional alternatives
without asking customers to compromise on efficacy, sustainability, or ethics.

BRAND VOICE
Verde communicates with confident, quiet authority. We let our certifications
and ingredient provenance do the heavy lifting. We do not shout. We do not preach.

FOUR PILLARS:
1. Transparent: We share supply chain data openly.
2. Evidence-based: Every claim is substantiated. We cite USDA certification.
3. Warm but not gushing: Real enthusiasm, not performative enthusiasm.
4. Direct without being blunt: No filler, no corporate hedging.

FORBIDDEN LANGUAGE
Never use: "best," "revolutionary," "toxic chemicals," "clinically proven"
(without citation), "miracle," "transforms overnight," "OMG."

VISUAL IDENTITY
Primary palette: Forest green (#2D5A27), warm white (#F8F4EF), earth brown (#6B4F3A)
Typography: Canela (headlines), GT America (body)
Photography: Natural light always. No heavy retouching.

LEGAL MANDATORIES
- USDA Organic seal on all paid media
- Legal disclaimer on any efficacy claims
- No before/after imagery (FDA guideline compliance)
- Brand tagline "Real Ingredients. Real Results." in all hero assets

TONE BY CHANNEL
Instagram: Slightly warmer, more personal. Community-forward.
Pinterest: Aspirational but grounded. Always link to ingredient sourcing.
Email: Most personal channel. Direct address. Highest information density.
Display: Ultra-concise. Lead with certification or proof point. No hype.
CTV/Video: Let visuals carry emotion. Copy provides evidence, not enthusiasm.
